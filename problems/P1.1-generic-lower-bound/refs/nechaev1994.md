# Nechaev 1994

- [CITED] Nechaev studies deterministic discrete-log algorithms in a model
  allowing group operations and equality tests but no representation-specific
  operations.
- [CITED] The resulting lower bound is $\Omega(\sqrt r)$ group operations in a
  prime-order group.

Primary source: V. I. Nechaev, Mathematical Notes 55(2), 1994, pp. 165–172,
DOI `10.1007/BF02113297`.
