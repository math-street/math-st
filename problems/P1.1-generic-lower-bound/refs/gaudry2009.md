# Gaudry 2009

- [CITED] Gaudry gives an index-calculus algorithm for small-dimensional
  abelian varieties and applies it to elliptic curves over small-degree
  extension fields.
- [CITED] The method uses factor-base decomposition, coordinate polynomial
  systems, extension/base-field structure, and relation linear algebra.

Primary source: Pierrick Gaudry, Journal of Symbolic Computation 44(12), 2009,
pp. 1690–1702, DOI `10.1016/j.jsc.2008.08.005`.
