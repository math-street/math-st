# Diem 2011

- [CITED] The factor base for $E/\mathbb F_{q^n}$ is defined through a
  degree-two covering whose value lies in $\mathbb P^1(\mathbb F_q)$.
- [CITED] Decomposition expands summation-polynomial equations in an
  $\mathbb F_q$-basis and solves a multivariate system over $\mathbb F_q$.
- [CITED] Relation collection and modular linear algebra recover the target
  logarithm directly in the elliptic-curve group.

Primary source: Claus Diem, Compositio Mathematica 147(1), 2011; author
preprint `https://www.math.uni-leipzig.de/~diem/preprints/dlp-ell-curves.pdf`.
