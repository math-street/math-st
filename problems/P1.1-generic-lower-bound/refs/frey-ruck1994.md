# Frey–Rück 1994

- [CITED] Frey and Rück reduce the DLP in an $m$-torsion divisor-class
  subgroup to a DLP in a field containing the $m$th roots of unity.
- [CITED] The reduction uses a Tate-pairing variant rather than a $p$-adic lift
  or multivariate decomposition system.

Primary source: Gerhard Frey and Hans-Georg Rück, Mathematics of Computation
62(206), 1994, pp. 865–874,
DOI `10.1090/S0025-5718-1994-1218343-6`.
