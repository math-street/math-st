# Semaev 1998

- [CITED] Semaev gives an $O(\log p)$-field-operation algorithm for a subgroup
  of order $p$ on an elliptic curve in characteristic $p$.
- [CITED] The construction uses coordinate arithmetic beyond an opaque group
  interface.

Primary source: Igor Semaev, Mathematics of Computation 67(221), 1998,
pp. 353–356, DOI `10.1090/S0025-5718-98-00887-4`.
