# Semaev 2004

- [CITED] The paper defines elliptic-curve summation polynomials and proposes
  relation generation by solving their zeros with bounded coordinate values.
- [CITED] The proposal uses those relations as the input to an index-calculus
  linear-algebra phase.
- [CITED] Its prime-field efficiency depends on obtaining sufficiently fast
  solutions to the bounded multivariate polynomial problem.

Primary source: Igor Semaev, IACR ePrint 2004/031,
`https://eprint.iacr.org/2004/031.pdf`.
