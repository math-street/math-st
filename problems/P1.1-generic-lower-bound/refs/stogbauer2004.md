# Stögbauer 2004

- [CITED] Appendix B gives a complete reduced Tate-pairing computation on
  $E:y^2=x^3+x$ over $\mathbb F_{43}$ with subgroup order 11, embedding degree
  2, $P=(23,8)$, and the distortion-map point $Q=(20,8t)$.
- [CITED] The appendix records the Miller sequence double, double, add, double,
  add and the reduced values $t(P,Q)=11+3t$ and
  $t([2]P,Q)=26+23t$.
- [PROVED] The local implementation retains vertical-line denominators, so its
  raw Miller representatives can differ from the appendix by a nonzero
  $\mathbb F_{43}$ factor; final exponentiation removes that factor and matches
  both reduced values exactly.

Primary source: Marcus Stögbauer, *Efficient Algorithms for Pairing-Based
Cryptosystems*, diploma thesis, Darmstadt University of Technology, Department
of Mathematics, 4 January 2004, Appendix B, pp. 67–69.
