# Smart 1999

- [CITED] Smart gives an elementary discrete-log algorithm for trace-one
  elliptic curves.
- [CITED] The paper states that curves whose group order equals the field order
  must be excluded from cryptographic use.

Primary source: Nigel P. Smart, Journal of Cryptology 12(3), 1999,
pp. 193–196, DOI `10.1007/s001459900052`.
