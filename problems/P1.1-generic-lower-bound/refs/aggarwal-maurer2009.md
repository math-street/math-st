# Aggarwal–Maurer 2009

- [CITED] The generic ring model exposes opaque ring elements through ring
  operations, inverse operations, and equality tests.
- [CITED] The paper applies that interface to the relation between RSA
  inversion and factoring, not directly to elliptic-curve points.

Primary source: Divesh Aggarwal and Ueli Maurer, EUROCRYPT 2009; full version
IACR ePrint 2008/260, `https://eprint.iacr.org/2008/260.pdf`.
