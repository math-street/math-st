# Menezes–Okamoto–Vanstone 1993

- [CITED] The paper reduces an elliptic-curve logarithm to a logarithm in the
  multiplicative group of a finite extension field.
- [CITED] The reduction uses the Weil pairing, extension-field arithmetic, and
  an auxiliary finite-field DLP.

Primary source: Alfred Menezes, Tatsuaki Okamoto, and Scott Vanstone, IEEE
Transactions on Information Theory 39(5), 1993, pp. 1639–1646,
DOI `10.1109/18.259647`.
