# Xylouris 2011

Triantafyllos Xylouris, “On the least prime in an arithmetic progression and estimates for the zeros of Dirichlet L-functions,” *Acta Arithmetica* 150(1), 65–91, 2011.

- [CITED] The paper proves an explicit Linnik-type fixed-power upper bound for the least prime in a reduced residue class.
- [PROVED] Applied to the class \(-1\bmod 4r\), this ensures that A002's least admissible curve prime has bit length \(O(\log r)\).
- [PROVED] A002 uses the result only for bit-length existence; its oracle setup may non-uniformly store the chosen prime and torsion data.
