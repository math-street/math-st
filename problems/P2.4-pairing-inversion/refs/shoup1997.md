# Shoup 1997

Victor Shoup, “Lower Bounds for Discrete Logarithms and Related Problems,” EUROCRYPT 1997, LNCS 1233, pp. 256–266.

- [CITED] The paper formalizes generic cyclic groups through random encodings and derives a quadratic-over-group-order success bound from collisions among formal expressions.
- [PROVED] P2.4/A002 specializes the same collision method to affine target expressions in a typed bilinear oracle with a free \(\mathbb G_2\)-DLP oracle.
- [PROVED] The specialization does not cover concrete target-field addition or representation-dependent algorithms.
