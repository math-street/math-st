# Conrad 2012 - affine groups over perfect fields

**Source:** Brian Conrad, "Finiteness Theorems for Algebraic Groups over
Function Fields," *Compositio Mathematica* 148(2), 555--639, 2012.

**URL:** <https://math.stanford.edu/~conrad/papers/cosetfinite.pdf>

**Checked result:** [CITED] The introduction records that a smooth connected
affine group over a perfect field has a unique exact sequence
$1\to U\to G\to G/U\to1$ with $U$ smooth, connected, split unipotent and
$G/U$ reductive.

**Use here:** [PROVED] When $G$ is commutative, the reductive quotient is a
torus.  A split unipotent group has a filtration by additive groups and is an
affine space as a variety, hence has $q^{\dim U}$ points over $\mathbb F_q$.
