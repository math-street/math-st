# Cantor 1987 - hyperelliptic Jacobian arithmetic

**Source:** David G. Cantor, "Computing in the Jacobian of a Hyperelliptic
Curve," *Mathematics of Computation* 48(177), 95--101, 1987.

**DOI:** <https://doi.org/10.1090/S0025-5718-1987-0866101-0>

**Checked result:** [CITED] The paper gives algorithms for reduction and group
arithmetic on divisor-class representations in a hyperelliptic Jacobian.

**Use here:** [PROVED] At fixed genus two, Mumford representations have
constant size and Cantor arithmetic uses bounded-degree polynomial and field
operations, so an explicit genus-two Jacobian has the algebraic group
interface needed by Maurer--Wolf.
