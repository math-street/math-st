# Conrad 2002 - Chevalley's structure theorem

**Source:** Brian Conrad, "A Modern Proof of Chevalley's Theorem on Algebraic
Groups," *Journal of the Ramanujan Mathematical Society* 17(1), 1--18,
2002.

**URL:** <https://math.stanford.edu/~conrad/papers/chev.pdf>

**Checked result:** [CITED] Theorem 1.1 states that over a perfect field every
smooth connected algebraic group $G$ has a unique normal connected affine
algebraic subgroup $H$ such that $G/H$ is an abelian variety.  Formation of
$H$ commutes with perfect field extension.

**Use here:** [PROVED] Over the finite field $\mathbb F_r$, this separates a
full connected commutative auxiliary group into its affine part and abelian-
variety quotient.
