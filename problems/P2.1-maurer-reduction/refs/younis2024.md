## Results relevant to P2.1

[CITED] Theorem 1.1 proves a smooth-number asymptotic for fixed interval
exponent $\theta>17/30$, but its lower smoothness range is
$\exp(C(\log x)^{2/3}(\log\log x)^{4/3})$, not a power of $\log x$.

[CITED] Under the Riemann Hypothesis, Theorem 1.3 reaches
$y=(\log x)^{K(\theta)}$ for each fixed $\theta>1/2$.

[PROVED] A Hasse interval has length $x^{1/2+o(1)}$, at the excluded endpoint
$\theta=1/2$.  Thus even the conditional polylogarithmic statement in this
paper does not supply a smooth integer in every Hasse interval.
