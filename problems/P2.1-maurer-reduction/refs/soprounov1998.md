## Result used in P2.1

[CITED] Theorem 2.1 states, for fixed coprime $a,q$,

$$
  \pi(x;q,a)\sim \frac{x}{\varphi(q)\log x}.
$$

[PROVED] For every fixed modulus $q$, subtracting the asymptotics at $2X$ and
$X$ shows that every reduced residue class contains a prime in $(X,2X)$ for
all sufficiently large $X$.  A finite collection of fixed residue classes
therefore supplies distinct comparable primes after taking $X$ large enough.
