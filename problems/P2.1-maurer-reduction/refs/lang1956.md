# Lang 1956 - algebraic groups over finite fields

**Source:** Serge Lang, "Algebraic Groups over Finite Fields," *American
Journal of Mathematics* 78(3), 555--563, 1956.

**DOI:** <https://doi.org/10.2307/2372673>

**Checked result:** [CITED] Lang's theorem makes the Lang map surjective for a
connected algebraic group over a finite field; equivalently, torsors under a
smooth connected finite-field algebraic group are trivial and
$H^1(\mathbb F_q,G)=1$.

**Use here:** [PROVED] For an exact sequence of smooth connected finite-field
groups, the rational-point map to the quotient is surjective, so the finite
rational-point orders multiply by the kernel order.
