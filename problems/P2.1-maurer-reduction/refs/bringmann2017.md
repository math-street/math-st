# Bringmann 2017

[CITED] Karl Bringmann, "A Near-Linear Pseudopolynomial Time Algorithm for
Subset Sum," in *Proceedings of the Twenty-Eighth Annual ACM--SIAM Symposium
on Discrete Algorithms (SODA 2017)*, 1073--1084, DOI
10.1137/1.9781611974782.69; arXiv:1610.04712.

Primary text checked at:
<https://arxiv.org/abs/1610.04712>

## Result relevant to P2.1

[CITED] For $n$ positive integer weights and numerical target $t$, the paper
gives a randomized algorithm with running time $\widetilde O(n+t)$.  The
dependence is near-linear in $t$ and hence pseudopolynomial rather than
polynomial in the bit length $\log t$.
