# Kani 1997

Ernst Kani, "The Number of Curves of Genus Two with Elliptic Differentials,"
*Journal für die reine und angewandte Mathematik* 485 (1997), pp. 93--122.

## Result used by P3.4

[CITED] Theorem 2.6 characterizes when an anti-isometry used to glue two
elliptic curves is reducible; the 2022 surface attacks restate it as the
criterion forcing a product codomain for an isogeny-diamond configuration.

## Scope

[PROVED] P3.4 relies on the attack papers' finite-field restatements and
algorithms, not on an independent reconstruction of all of Kani's geometric
proof.

**Proof.** Every positive classifier branch cites a theorem or algorithm in
Castryck--Decru, Maino--Martindale, or Robert; none invokes Kani 1997 directly
as executable code.
