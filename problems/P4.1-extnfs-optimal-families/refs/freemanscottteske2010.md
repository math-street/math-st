# Freeman–Scott–Teske 2010

[CITED] David Freeman, Michael Scott, and Edlyn Teske, “A Taxonomy of Pairing-Friendly Elliptic Curves,” *Journal of Cryptology* 23, 224–280, 2010, doi:10.1007/s00145-009-9048-z; IACR ePrint 2006/372.

## Main result in repository notation

[CITED] The paper organizes complete and sparse pairing-friendly constructions in a common framework and compares families across embedding degrees up to 50.

## Relevance

[CITED] The taxonomy supplies the broader construction context in which BN, BLS, and KSS are only a small selected search space.
