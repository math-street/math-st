# Guillevic 2020

[CITED] Aurore Guillevic, “A Short-List of Pairing-Friendly Curves Resistant to Special TNFS at the 128-bit Security Level,” PKC 2020; IACR ePrint 2019/1371.

## Main result in repository notation

[CITED] The paper refines special-TNFS polynomial selection for polynomially parameterized characteristics and revises practical 128-bit family comparisons.

[CITED] Its efficiency-oriented shortlist favors BLS12 or related $k=12$ families over approximately 440–448-bit base fields at the 128-bit level.

## Relevance

[CITED] This result is a practical counterpoint to minimizing $\rho$ alone and provides a direct explanation for why the P4.1 formal optimum need not be the deployment optimum.
