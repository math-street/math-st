# Bernstein-Lange-Schwabe 2011

[CITED] Daniel J. Bernstein, Tanja Lange, and Peter Schwabe, "On the Correct
Use of the Negation Map in the Pollard Rho Method," PKC 2011; expanded
version IACR ePrint 2011/003. <https://eprint.iacr.org/2011/003>

## Relevance

- [CITED] The paper gives a branchless formulation that fully exploits negation-map equivalence classes in a practical parallel setting.
- [CITED] It is evidence that cycle handling and machine architecture affect the realized constant even when the quotient-space factor is fixed.
