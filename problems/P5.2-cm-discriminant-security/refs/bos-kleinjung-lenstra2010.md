# Bos-Kleinjung-Lenstra 2010

[CITED] Joppe W. Bos, Thorsten Kleinjung, and Arjen K. Lenstra, "On the Use
of the Negation Map in the Pollard Rho Method," ANTS 2010, LNCS 6197,
67-83, doi:10.1007/978-3-642-14518-6_9.

## Relevance

- [CITED] The paper analyzes recurring fruitless cycles in negation-map rho and evaluates countermeasures.
- [CITED] It establishes that quotient cardinality alone does not determine implementable rho speedup.
