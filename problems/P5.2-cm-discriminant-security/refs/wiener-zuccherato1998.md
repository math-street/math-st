# Wiener-Zuccherato 1998

[CITED] Michael J. Wiener and Robert J. Zuccherato, "Faster Attacks on
Elliptic Curve Cryptosystems," Selected Areas in Cryptography 1998, LNCS
1556, 190-200.

## Relevance

- [CITED] The paper is early prior art for defining elliptic-curve rho walks on efficiently recognized equivalence classes.
- [CITED] Later cycle analyses attribute the look-ahead countermeasure for short fruitless cycles to this work (Wang-Zhang 2011, ePrint 2011/008).
