# Wang-Zhang 2011

[CITED] Ping Wang and Fangguo Zhang, "Computing Elliptic Curve Discrete
Logarithms with the Negation Map," IACR ePrint 2011/008.
<https://eprint.iacr.org/2011/008.pdf>

## Relevance

- [CITED] The paper derives probabilities for fruitless $2t$-cycles in an $r$-adding negation-map walk.
- [CITED] It proposes block-minimum cycle detection and a deterministic doubling escape and reports speed close to the ideal $\sqrt2$ factor.
- [CITED] It states the ideal-random-mapping first-repeat estimate $\sqrt{\pi N/2}$ used in the ceiling calculation.
