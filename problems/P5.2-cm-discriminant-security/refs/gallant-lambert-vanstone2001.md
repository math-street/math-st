# Gallant-Lambert-Vanstone 2001

[CITED] Robert P. Gallant, Robert J. Lambert, and Scott A. Vanstone,
"Faster Point Multiplication on Elliptic Curves with Efficient
Endomorphisms," CRYPTO 2001, LNCS 2139, 190-200.
<https://www.iacr.org/archive/crypto2001/21390189.pdf>

## Relevance

- [CITED] The paper gives the explicit $j=1728$ map $(x,y)\mapsto(-x,\alpha y)$ and the $j=0$ map $(x,y)\mapsto(\beta x,y)$ over suitable prime fields.
- [CITED] On an invariant prime-order cyclic subgroup, each efficient endomorphism acts as scalar multiplication by a root of its quadratic characteristic polynomial modulo the subgroup order.
- [CITED] The GLV lattice decomposition accelerates scalar multiplication; it does not claim a subexponential ECDLP algorithm.
