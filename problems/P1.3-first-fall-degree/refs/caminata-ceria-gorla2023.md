# Caminata, Ceria, and Gorla (2023)

Alessio Caminata, Michela Ceria, and Elisa Gorla, “The complexity of solving
Weil restriction systems,” *Journal of Algebra* 621 (2023), 116–133.
DOI: 10.1016/j.jalgebra.2023.01.008; arXiv:2112.10506.

## Results relevant to P1.3

[CITED] The paper formalizes coordinate Weil restriction and notes the natural
bijection between the extension-field solutions of the original system and
the base-field solutions of the restricted system.

[CITED] Theorem 4.7 bounds solving degree for nonhomogeneous Weil-restriction
systems using Castelnuovo–Mumford regularity of a homogenized source system;
Corollary 4.12 treats the addition of field equations.

## Boundary of the citation

[CITED] These are general invariant bounds. Converting them into a sharp,
explicit function of the Semaev parameters $q,n,m$ still requires control of
the relevant source-system regularity, which the local experiments do not
provide.
