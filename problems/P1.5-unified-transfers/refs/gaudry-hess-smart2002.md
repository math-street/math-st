# Gaudry--Hess--Smart 2002

- [CITED] Pierrick Gaudry, Florian Hess, and Nigel P. Smart, "Constructive and
  Destructive Facets of Weil Descent on Elliptic Curves," *Journal of
  Cryptology* 15(1), 19--46, 2002, doi:10.1007/s00145-001-0011-x.
- [CITED] The paper constructs Weil-descent homomorphisms from selected binary
  elliptic-curve groups to divisor-class groups over smaller fields.
- [PROVED] Under SG-01, an instance counts only when the subgroup kernel and
  target asymptotic DLP cost are also checked.
