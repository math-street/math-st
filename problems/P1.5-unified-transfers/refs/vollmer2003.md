# Vollmer 2003

- [CITED] Ulrich Vollmer, *Rigorously Analyzed Algorithms for the Discrete
  Logarithm Problem in Quadratic Number Fields*, doctoral dissertation,
  Technische Universität Darmstadt, submitted 2003.
- [CITED] Lemma 4.16 and Algorithm 4.1 give polynomial-time binary power
  products of reduced imaginary-quadratic ideals. Algorithm 4.2 and Lemma
  4.17 record every relative reduction generator in a compact power-product
  representation whose number of components is logarithmic in the
  exponents.
- [PROVED] A028 uses the sign-independent bookkeeping idea, together with
  ordinary imaginary-quadratic reduction, to retain a compact generator of
  \(\mathfrak a^r\). It does not claim Vollmer's real-infrastructure
  algorithm as new.
