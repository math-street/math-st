# Semaev 1998

- [CITED] Igor A. Semaev, "Evaluation of Discrete Logarithms in a Group of
  $p$-Torsion Points of an Elliptic Curve in Characteristic $p$,"
  *Mathematics of Computation* 67(221), 353--356, 1998,
  doi:10.1090/S0025-5718-98-00887-4.
- [CITED] The paper gives an $O(\log p)$ field-operation algorithm for a
  subgroup of order $p$ over characteristic $p$.
- [PROVED] The result shows that the additive family is broader than only the
  prime-field equation $\#E(\mathbb F_p)=p$ used in the problem prompt.
