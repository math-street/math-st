# Soleng 1994

- [CITED] Ragnar Soleng, "Homomorphisms from the Group of Rational Points on
  Elliptic Curves to Class Groups of Quadratic Number Fields," *Journal of
  Number Theory* 46(2), 214--229, 1994,
  doi:10.1006/jnth.1994.1013.
- [CITED] Gillibert (2018) identifies Soleng's construction as a
  specialization of a degree-zero line bundle on an elliptic curve over
  \(\mathbb Q\) at quadratic points and relates it to the Mazur--Tate class
  group pairing.
- [PROVED] The source points and the target quadratic fields are
  characteristic-zero arithmetic objects, not a direct map from
  \(E(\mathbb F_q)\) to one fixed number-field class group.
