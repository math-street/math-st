# Belding 2007

- [CITED] Juliana V. Belding, "A Weil pairing on the $p$-torsion of ordinary
  elliptic curves over the dual numbers of $K$," arXiv:math/0703906, 2007.
- [CITED] The paper constructs a nondegenerate Weil-pairing analogue on
  $p$-torsion over dual numbers.
- [CITED] It interprets the Semaev--Ruck characteristic-$p$ DLP attacks as
  pairing attacks analogous to MOV and gives a dual-number anomalous attack.
- [PROVED] This is the direct literature bridge supporting the common framework
  in `NOTES.md` SG-04.
