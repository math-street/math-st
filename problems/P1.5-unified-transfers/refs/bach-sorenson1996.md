# Bach--Sorenson 1996

- [CITED] Eric Bach and Jonathan Sorenson, "Explicit Bounds for Primes in
  Residue Classes," *Mathematics of Computation* 65(216), 1717--1735,
  1996.
- [CITED] Assuming ERH/GRH, the paper gives a degree-one prime with prescribed
  Artin symbol of norm
  \((1+o(1))(\log|\Delta_L|+2[L:\mathbb Q])^2\), and notes the arbitrary
  Galois/conjugacy-class form.
- [PROVED] Combined with A028's
  \([L:\mathbb Q]=r^{O(1)}\) and
  \(\log|\Delta_L|=r^{O(1)}(B+\log r)\) bounds, this makes the separating
  auxiliary prime have \(O(\log r+\log(B+2))\) bits.
