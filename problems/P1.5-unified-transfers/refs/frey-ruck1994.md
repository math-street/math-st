# Frey--Ruck 1994

- [CITED] Gerhard Frey and Hans-Georg Ruck, "A Remark Concerning
  $m$-Divisibility and the Discrete Logarithm in the Divisor Class Group of
  Curves," *Mathematics of Computation* 62(206), 865--874, 1994,
  doi:10.1090/S0025-5718-1994-1218343-6.
- [CITED] The paper uses a Tate-pairing variant to reduce prime-to-characteristic
  torsion logarithms in divisor class groups to finite-field multiplicative
  logarithms.
