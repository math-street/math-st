# Poonen 2021

- [CITED] Bjorn Poonen, *Introduction to Drinfeld Modules*, expository notes
  dated December 29, 2021.
- [CITED] Definition 3.2 defines a Drinfeld $A$-module over $L$ as the additive
  group scheme $\mathbb G_a$ equipped with a faithful $A$-module structure.
- [PROVED] Over a finite field of characteristic $p$, this underlying additive
  group has exponent $p$, which supplies the elementary exclusion used in the
  P1.5 candidate table.
