# Xylouris 2011

- [CITED] Triantafyllos Xylouris, "On the Least Prime in an Arithmetic
  Progression and Estimates for the Zeros of Dirichlet L-Functions,"
  *Acta Arithmetica* 150(1), 65--91, 2011; arXiv:0906.2749.
- [CITED] The paper proves that $5.2$ is an admissible Linnik exponent: the
  least prime in any reduced residue class modulo $q$ is at most
  $Cq^{5.2}$ for an effective absolute constant $C$.
- [PROVED] Applied to the CRT class
  $p\equiv3\pmod4$, $p\equiv-1\pmod r$ modulo $4r$, this gives the succinct
  infinite family used in A025, with $\log p=O(\log r)$.

Primary sources: <https://arxiv.org/abs/0906.2749> and
<https://eudml.org/doc/279129>.
