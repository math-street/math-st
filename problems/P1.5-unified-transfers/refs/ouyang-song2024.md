# Ouyang--Song 2024

- [CITED] Yi Ouyang and Qimin Song, "Divisibility of Class Numbers of
  Quadratic Fields and a Conjecture of Iizuka," arXiv:2406.05975, 2024.
- [CITED] For fixed positive integers \(x,n\), the paper proves that the class
  number of \(\mathbb Q(\sqrt{x^2-y^n})\) is divisible by odd \(n\) for
  coprime sufficiently large \(y\).
- [CITED] Its threshold depends on \((x,n)\); the proof invokes Siegel
  finiteness to define a constant and does not make that threshold effective.
- [PROVED] Consequently the checked theorem is an existence result, not a
  uniform succinct target constructor with \(n\) supplied as input.
