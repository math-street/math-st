# Blum--Choi--Hoey--Iskander--Lakein--Martinez 2022

- [CITED] Talia Blum, Caroline Choi, Alexandra Hoey, Jonas Iskander, Kaya
  Lakein, and Thomas C. Martinez, "On Class Numbers, Torsion Subgroups, and
  Quadratic Twists of Elliptic Curves," *Transactions of the American
  Mathematical Society* 375, 351--368, 2022,
  doi:10.1090/tran/8457; arXiv:2007.08756.
- [CITED] For suitable integers \((u,v)\), the paper constructs explicit
  homomorphisms
  \(\Phi_{u,v}:E(\mathbb Q)\to\operatorname{Cl}(-D_E(u,v))\).
- [PROVED] This strengthens the checked evidence that explicit point-to-class
  maps are available in characteristic zero, but its domain is
  \(E(\mathbb Q)\), not \(E(\mathbb F_q)\).
