# Fischler--Nakamaye - interpolation on commutative algebraic groups

- [CITED] Stéphane Fischler and Michael Nakamaye, “Seshadri Constants and
  Interpolation on Commutative Algebraic Groups,” *Annales de l'Institut
  Fourier* 64(3), 1269--1289, 2014, doi:10.5802/aif.2880.
- [CITED] The paper develops finite-set interpolation estimates and
  obstruction subgroups on compactifications of connected commutative
  algebraic groups, refining Masser and Fischler.
- [CITED] Its prior-art discussion says that torsion-only sets disappear from
  some earlier interpolation estimates.
- [PROVED] It is relevant background but does not state P1.5's
  prime-subgroup homomorphism-defect theorem or its piecewise branch tradeoff.

Primary source: <https://arxiv.org/abs/1205.4088>
