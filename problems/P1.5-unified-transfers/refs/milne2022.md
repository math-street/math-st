# Milne 2022

- [CITED] James S. Milne, *Algebraic Groups: The Theory of Group Schemes of
  Finite Type over a Field*, version 2.00, 2022.
- [CITED] Sections 2 and 8 record that complete connected group varieties are
  abelian varieties and that homomorphisms from anti-affine groups to affine
  algebraic groups are trivial.
- [PROVED] These facts, plus dimension of the image, yield the restricted
  classification theorem proved in `NOTES.md` SG-07.
