# Conrad - A modern proof of Chevalley's theorem

- [CITED] Brian Conrad, “A Modern Proof of Chevalley's Theorem on Algebraic
  Groups,” dated April 2, 2002.
- [CITED] Theorem 1.1 states that over a perfect field, every connected smooth
  algebraic group has a unique connected affine normal subgroup with abelian
  variety quotient.
- [PROVED] The P1.5 mixed-target theorem uses this only for the identity
  component over a finite, hence perfect, field.

Primary source: <https://math.stanford.edu/~conrad/papers/chev.pdf>
