# Buell--Call 2016

- [CITED] Duncan A. Buell and Gregory S. Call, "Class pairings and isogenies
  on elliptic curves," *Journal of Number Theory* 167, 31--73, 2016,
  doi:10.1016/j.jnt.2016.02.030.
- [CITED] The paper gives explicit local-symbol formulas for elliptic class
  pairings over number fields and identifies Buell's earlier point-to-class
  homomorphism as a fixed-argument instance.
- [CITED] Theorem 4.4 and Corollary 4.5 relate the class pairing with fixed
  torsion input to a Weil-descent pairing followed by a valuation/class map.
- [PROVED] The cited domain and target are global arithmetic objects; A005
  records why the direct finite-field or local-base specialization has trivial
  Picard target.
