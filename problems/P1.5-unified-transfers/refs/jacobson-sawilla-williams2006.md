# Jacobson--Sawilla--Williams 2006

- [CITED] Michael J. Jacobson, Jr., Reginald E. Sawilla, and Hugh C.
  Williams, "Efficient Ideal Reduction in Quadratic Fields,"
  *International Journal of Mathematics and Computer Science* 1 (2006),
  83--116.
- [CITED] The paper gives ideal-reduction algorithms uniformly for positive
  and negative discriminants and explicitly tracks relative generators.
- [PROVED] A028 uses this as the computational reduction input behind the
  compact generator of a principal ideal power; the Kummer character and the
  target-factorization theorem are not results of this paper.
