# Winterhof 2002 and the requested Meidl--Winterhof attribution

- [CITED] The bibliographic item titled "Polynomial Interpolation of the
  Discrete Logarithm" is Arne Winterhof, *Designs, Codes and Cryptography*
  25(1), 63--72, 2002, doi:10.1023/A:1012556500517. Crossref, DBLP, and the
  journal metadata list Winterhof as the sole author.
- [CITED] The paper extends most Coppersmith--Shparlinski prime-field
  interpolation bounds to arbitrary finite fields $\mathbb F_{p^m}$.
- [CITED] The distinct 2002 Meidl--Winterhof paper is Wilfried Meidl and Arne
  Winterhof, "A Polynomial Representation of the Diffie--Hellman Mapping,"
  *Applicable Algebra in Engineering, Communication and Computing* 13(4),
  313--318, doi:10.1007/s00200-002-0104-2.
- [PROVED] P1.5 cites the sole-authored discrete-logarithm paper for the
  requested interpolation line and records the second paper to prevent the
  two records from being conflated.

Primary sources: <https://doi.org/10.1023/A:1012556500517> and
