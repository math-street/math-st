# Milne - Jacobian Varieties

- [CITED] James S. Milne, *Jacobian Varieties*, online notes, revised version.
- [CITED] Theorem 1.1 constructs the Jacobian $J_C$ and identifies
  $J_C(k)$ with $\operatorname{Pic}^0(C)$ when the complete nonsingular curve
  $C/k$ has a $k$-rational point.
- [CITED] Section 9 defines the generalized Jacobian relative to a modulus by
  degree-zero divisor classes prime to the modulus modulo the prescribed
  principal congruence relation.
- [PROVED] A014 uses these results to place global function-field ideal and ray
  class targets inside the ordinary/generalized Jacobian branches.

Primary source: <https://www.jmilne.org/math/xnotes/JVs.pdf>
