# Breen--Varma--Voight 2023

- [CITED] Benjamin Breen, Ila Varma, and John Voight, with an appendix by
  Noam Elkies, "On Unit Signatures and Narrow Class Groups of Odd Degree
  Abelian Number Fields," *Transactions of the American Mathematical
  Society, Series B* 10 (2023), 86--128.
- [CITED] Section 2 records the number-field Selmer exact sequences and the
  Kummer pairing between class-group quotients and virtual units explicitly;
  the paper's main application is the \(2\)-Selmer/signature setting.
- [PROVED] This is direct conceptual prior art for A028's virtual-unit and
  power-residue ingredients. A028 claims only the prime-order
  quadratic-target computational factorization assembled from those
  classical ingredients.
