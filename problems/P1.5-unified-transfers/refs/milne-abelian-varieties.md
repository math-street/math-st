# Milne - Abelian Varieties

- [CITED] James S. Milne, *Abelian Varieties*, course notes, version 2.00,
  2008, with later online corrections.
- [CITED] Corollary 1.2 states that every regular map of abelian varieties is a
  homomorphism followed by a translation; in particular, a map preserving zero
  is a homomorphism.  The proof uses the rigidity lemma.
- [PROVED] A011 combines this with proper-target extension to show that a
  rational subgroup homomorphism $E\dashrightarrow A$ is automatically a
  global homomorphism.

Primary source: <https://www.jmilne.org/math/CourseNotes/AV110.pdf>
