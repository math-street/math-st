# Stacks Project - Picard groups of rings

- [CITED] The Stacks Project Authors, Section 15.119, "Picard groups of
  rings," defines the Picard group through finite locally free rank-one
  modules.
- [CITED] Lemma 10.153.10 records the finite-product decomposition into local
  factors used for zero-dimensional finite algebras.
- [PROVED] Combining that decomposition with the elementary fact that every
  invertible module over a local ring is free proves
  $\operatorname{Pic}(R)=0$ for every finite commutative ring.

Primary sources: <https://stacks.math.columbia.edu/tag/0AFW> and
