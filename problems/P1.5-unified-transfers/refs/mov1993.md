# Menezes--Okamoto--Vanstone 1993

- [CITED] Alfred J. Menezes, Tatsuaki Okamoto, and Scott A. Vanstone, "Reducing
  Elliptic Curve Logarithms to Logarithms in a Finite Field," *IEEE
  Transactions on Information Theory* 39(5), 1639--1646, 1993,
  doi:10.1109/18.259647.
- [CITED] The paper reduces suitable elliptic-curve subgroup DLPs to finite
  extension-field DLPs through a nondegenerate torsion pairing.

  pairing implementation recovers every fixed logarithm in the experiment
  matrix.
