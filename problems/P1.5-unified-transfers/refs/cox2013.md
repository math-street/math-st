# Cox 2013

- [CITED] David A. Cox, *Primes of the Form $x^2+ny^2$: Fermat, Class Field
  Theory, and Complex Multiplication*, second edition, Wiley, 2013.
- [CITED] Theorem 7.24 gives the class-number formula for an imaginary
  quadratic order in terms of the maximal-order class number, conductor, unit
  index, and local Kronecker factors.
- [CITED] Jeremy Booher's 2014 notes "Modular curves and the class number one
  problem," Proposition 3, independently restate the same formula and point
  to Cox Section 7 for the proof.
- [PROVED] A003 combines this formula with an explicit elementary bound on
  $L(1,\chi)$ to obtain the endomorphism-order size obstruction.
