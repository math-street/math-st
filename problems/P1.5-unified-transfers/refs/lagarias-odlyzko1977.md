# Lagarias--Odlyzko 1977

- [CITED] Jeffrey C. Lagarias and Andrew M. Odlyzko, "Effective Versions of
  the Chebotarev Density Theorem," in *Algebraic Number Fields*, A.
  Fröhlich, ed., Academic Press, 1977, 409--464.
- [CITED] Under GRH for the relevant Dedekind zeta function, effective
  Chebotarev counts primes with prescribed Frobenius using an error governed
  polynomially by the extension degree and \(\log|\operatorname{Disc} L|\).
- [PROVED] A028 applies this only after bounding the degree and logarithmic
  discriminant of the normal Kummer closure. The unconditional algebraic
  existence of separating primes uses ordinary Chebotarev and does not
  require GRH.
