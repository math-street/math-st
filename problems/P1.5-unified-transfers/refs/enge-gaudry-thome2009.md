# Enge--Gaudry--Thome 2009

- [CITED] Andreas Enge, Pierrick Gaudry, and Emmanuel Thome, "An $L(1/3)$
  Discrete Logarithm Algorithm for Low Degree Curves," arXiv:0905.2177, 2009.
- [CONDITIONAL: the paper's curve-family restrictions and smoothness heuristics]
  DLPs in the selected high-genus low-degree Jacobian families take
  $L_{q^g}(1/3,O(1))$ time.
- [PROVED] High genus by itself is therefore not a sufficient target-side
  certificate; the family and heuristic hypotheses must be recorded.
