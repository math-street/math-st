# Milne 1997 - Class Field Theory

- [CITED] James S. Milne, *Class Field Theory*, version 3.1 course notes,
  1997.
- [CITED] Theorem V.1.5 gives the ray-class exact sequence, identifies its
  congruence term with $(\mathcal O_K/\mathfrak m_0)^\times$ (plus real signs),
  and gives the exact ray class number formula used in A004.
- [CITED] Chapter VI, Section 2 gives the Dedekind-zeta residue formula; in the
  imaginary quadratic case it specializes to the analytic class-number
  formula used in A003.
- [PROVED] Applying Theorem V.1.5 to $\mathbb Q(i)$ yields the modulus-$r$ and
  modulus-$r^2$ orders recorded by `code/probe_cm_class_targets.py`.
