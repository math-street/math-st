# Shoup 1997

- [CITED] Victor Shoup, "Lower Bounds for Discrete Logarithms and Related
  Problems," EUROCRYPT 1997, LNCS 1233, 256--266,
  doi:10.1007/3-540-69053-0_18.
- [CITED] In the random-injective-encoding generic-group model, the success
  probability of an algorithm using $m$ oracle calls against a prime-order
  discrete logarithm is $O(m^2/r)$.
- [PROVED] A007 applies this bound only after counting every source query made
  by transfer setup and evaluation and excluding encoding-dependent advice or
  an uncounted cross-representation oracle.

Primary source: <https://www.shoup.net/papers/dlbounds1.pdf>
