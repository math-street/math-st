# Lim 2016

- [CITED] Meng Fai Lim, "On the Divisibility of Class Numbers and
  Discriminants of Imaginary Quadratic Fields," arXiv:1601.05180, 2016.
- [CITED] For every squarefree odd integer \(n>1\), the paper constructs
  infinitely many imaginary quadratic fields whose class groups contain an
  element of order \(n\), while their discriminants are also divisible by
  \(n\).
- [CITED] The proof selects integers through congruence conditions and uses
  equations of the shape \(a^2-4b^n=c^2d\) together with an exact-order
  criterion.
- [PROVED] The theorem as stated supplies no polynomial-in-\(\log n\)
  discriminant bound or uniform polynomial-time construction required by
  SG-01.
