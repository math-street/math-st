# Hühnlein--Takagi 1999

- [CITED] Detlef Hühnlein and Tsuyoshi Takagi, "Reducing Logarithms in
  Totally Non-Maximal Imaginary Quadratic Orders to Logarithms in Finite
  Fields," ASIACRYPT 1999, LNCS 1716, 219--231.
- [CITED] For a nonmaximal imaginary quadratic order of prime conductor
  \(p\) whose maximal order has class number one, the paper reduces the
  class-group discrete logarithm problem in expected
  \(O(\log^3 p)\) bit operations to a DLP in
  \(\mathbb F_p^\times\) in the split case and
  \(\mathbb F_{p^2}^\times\) in the inert case.
- [PROVED] This is direct prior art for the finite-field interpretation of
  the tame conductor kernel. A026 therefore cannot claim that interpretation
  or the Gaussian specialization as a new mechanism.
