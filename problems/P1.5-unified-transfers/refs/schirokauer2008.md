# Schirokauer 2008

- [CITED] Oliver Schirokauer, "The Impact of the Number Field Sieve on the
  Discrete Logarithm Problem in Finite Fields," in *Algorithmic Number
  Theory*, MSRI Publications 44, 397--420, 2008.
- [CITED] The chapter describes NFS-derived discrete-logarithm algorithms for
  finite fields of prime-power order and the standard subexponential
  complexity convention used for fixed-degree large-characteristic targets.
- [PROVED] A025 reduces its ring-class image DLP by polynomial-time Gaussian
  ideal extension to the same $\mathbb F_{p^2}^{\times}$ DLP used by the
  established pairing transfer. It does not claim a faster target algorithm.
