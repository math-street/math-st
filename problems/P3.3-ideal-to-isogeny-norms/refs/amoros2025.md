# Amoros--Clements--Martindale 2025

**Reference:** Laia Amoros, James Clements, and Chloe Martindale,
"Parametrizing Maximal Orders Along Supersingular $\ell$-Isogeny Paths,"
IACR Cryptology ePrint Archive, Report 2025/033, 2025.

## Main result in repository notation

[CITED] For a specified family $p=4f\ell^e-1$ with
$\ell\equiv3\pmod4$, the paper explicitly parametrizes maximal orders reached
within $e$ steps of the order corresponding to $j=1728$ and gives associated
norm forms. [Theorem 3.1]

## What it leaves open

[CITED] The paper describes the path produced by the SQIsign KLPT subroutine as
far from optimally short and leaves using the parametrized norm forms for an
optimally short path as future work. [Introduction and Section 5.3]

## Repository verification

[PROVED] P3.3 uses neither the paper's restricted prime shape nor its order
parametrization, so no computation from this paper is claimed reproduced.
