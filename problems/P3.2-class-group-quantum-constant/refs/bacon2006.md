# Bacon–Childs–van Dam 2006

- [CITED] Dave Bacon, Andrew M. Childs, and Wim van Dam, “Optimal Measurements for the Dihedral Hidden Subgroup Problem,” Chicago Journal of Theoretical Computer Science 2006(2), DOI 10.4086/cjtcs.2006.002; arXiv:quant-ph/0501044.
- [CITED] The optimal measurement has a sharp threshold in the number of dihedral hidden-subgroup states: fewer than `log2 N` copies give exponentially small identification probability, while more give constant probability.
- [PROVED] `LOWER_BOUND.md` supplies a shorter dimension bound sufficient for `Omega(log N)` in the independent phase-state interface, but it does not reproduce the paper's sharp probability analysis.
