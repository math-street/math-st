# Kuperberg 2005

- [CITED] Greg Kuperberg, “A Subexponential-Time Quantum Algorithm for the Dihedral Hidden Subgroup Problem,” SIAM Journal on Computing 35 (2005), 170–188; arXiv:quant-ph/0302112.
- [CITED] The paper gives time and query complexity `exp(O(sqrt(log N)))` for the dihedral hidden-subgroup problem and extends the method to hidden shift over finitely generated abelian groups.
- [PROVED] The asymptotic statement does not select a physical gate set, error-correcting code, QRACM implementation, or finite-size leading constant; these items are not variables in the theorem statement.
- [PROVED] No numerical computation from this paper was used to support a repository result.
