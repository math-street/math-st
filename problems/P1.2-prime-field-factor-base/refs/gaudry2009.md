# Gaudry 2009

[CITED] Pierrick Gaudry, “Index calculus for abelian varieties of small
dimension and the elliptic curve discrete logarithm problem,” *Journal of
Symbolic Computation* 44(12), 1690–1702, 2009,
doi:10.1016/j.jsc.2008.08.005.
<https://doi.org/10.1016/j.jsc.2008.08.005>

## Relevance

[CITED] The paper applies an algebraic index-calculus construction to Weil
restrictions of elliptic and hyperelliptic curves over small-degree extension
fields.

[CITED] The stated extension-field complexity analysis is heuristic; it does
not supply the prime-field factor base sought in P1.2.
