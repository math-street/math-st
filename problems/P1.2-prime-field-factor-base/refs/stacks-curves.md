# Stacks Project — algebraic curves

[CITED] The Stacks Project Authors, *The Stacks Project*, Chapter 53,
“Algebraic Curves,” especially Lemma 53.2.2 (tag 0BXZ), Proposition 53.13.7
(tag 0CD2), and Lemma 53.12.2 (tag 0C1D).
<https://stacks.math.columbia.edu/chapter/curves>

## Relevance

[CITED] Lemma 53.2.2 extends rational maps from normal curves to proper targets;
Proposition 53.13.7 separates the inseparable Frobenius part of a curve
morphism; and Lemma 53.12.2 supplies Riemann–Hurwitz for the separable part.

[PROVED] Combining these cited results shows that a rational map
$\mathbb P^1\dashrightarrow E$ to an elliptic curve is constant.
