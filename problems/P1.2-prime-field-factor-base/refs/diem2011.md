# Diem 2011

[CITED] Claus Diem, “On the discrete logarithm problem in elliptic curves,”
*Compositio Mathematica* 147, 75–104, 2011,
doi:10.1112/S0010437X10005075.
<https://doi.org/10.1112/S0010437X10005075>

## Relevance

[CITED] For extension fields $\mathbb F_{q^n}$ in the paper's stated growing
regime, the paper proves subexponential expected-time ECDLP algorithms and
constructs the factor base using a covering whose value lies in
$\mathbb P^1(\mathbb F_q)$.

[CITED] The paper explicitly separates Semaev's bounded-coordinate prime-field
proposal from the extension-field decomposition mechanism; the latter relies
on the proper base field.
