# Belles-Munoz, Jimenez Urroz, and Silva (2022)

Marta Belles-Munoz, Jorge Jimenez Urroz, and Javier Silva, "Revisiting Cycles
of Pairing-Friendly Elliptic Curves," IACR ePrint 2022/1662, 2022.

Primary source: <https://eprint.iacr.org/2022/1662>

## Results used here

[CITED] The paper studies 2-cycles containing curves from known
prime-order polynomial families and gives explicit finite bounds for possible
partner embedding degrees.

[CITED] Table 2 records an exceptional MNT3 instance at parameter \(x=1\): a
curve over \(\mathbb F_{11}\) of order 7 and embedding degree 3 whose cycle
partner over \(\mathbb F_7\) has order 11 and embedding degree 10.

## Scope

[CITED] The paper's family-based exclusions do not enumerate every arbitrary
prime pair in a fixed finite interval. The P4.2 search is therefore a bounded
isogeny-class census, not a reproduction of the paper's polynomial argument.
