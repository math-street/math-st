# Buchmann--Hollinger 1996

[CITED] Johannes A. Buchmann and Christine S. Hollinger, “On Smooth Ideals in Number Fields,” *Journal of Number Theory* 59(1), 1996, pp. 82--87, DOI 10.1006/jnth.1996.0088.

[CITED] Primary-source abstract checked: <https://doi.org/10.1006/jnth.1996.0088>.

[CONDITIONAL: GRH] The paper gives a lower bound for the number of smooth ideals whose dependence outside the smoothness parameters is only on the number-field degree.

[PROVED] It does not provide an unconditional input for P4.3.  It also counts ideals rather than bounded principal generators shared by two exTNFS sides, so GRH alone would not discharge (RC) or (SQ).
