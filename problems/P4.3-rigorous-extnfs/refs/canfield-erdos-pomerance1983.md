# Canfield--Erdos--Pomerance 1983

[CITED] E. R. Canfield, Paul Erdos, and Carl Pomerance, “On a Problem of Oppenheim Concerning ‘Factorisatio Numerorum’,” *Journal of Number Theory* 17(1), 1983, pp. 1--28, DOI 10.1016/0022-314X(83)90002-1.

[CITED] Primary source metadata: <https://doi.org/10.1016/0022-314X(83)90002-1>.  The exact uniform form used here is also restated as Fact 3.6 and Corollary 3.7 in Lee--Venkatesan 2018.

[CITED] Relevant content: for $3\le u\le(1-\epsilon)\log x/\log\log x$, the paper gives a uniform asymptotic for $\Psi(x,x^{1/u})$.  The standard substitution $x=L_Q(b,d)$ and $y=L_Q(a,c)$ yields the random-integer $L$-density used in NFS analyses.

[PROVED] This theorem counts integers, not values of a polynomial or norm form.  It is therefore the benchmark in S-10 and does not establish S-01--S-08.
