# Schirokauer 2008

[CITED] Oliver Schirokauer, “The Number Field Sieve and the Discrete Logarithm Problem,” in *Surveys in Algorithmic Number Theory*, Mathematical Sciences Research Institute Publications 44, Cambridge University Press, 2008, pp. 397--420.

[CITED] Primary source checked: <https://library.slmath.org/books/Book44/files/12oliver.pdf>.

[CITED] Section 2 constructs the NFS-DLP valuation and logarithmic-map vectors.  In Step 4 it states that when the target vector is not in the column space, the sieve region is enlarged and the rank is expected to increase.

[CITED] The same section gives the quasiquadratic sparse linear-algebra cost after a solvable matrix is supplied.  It does not prove that the structured relation vectors achieve the required rank.
