# Gaudry–Hess–Smart 2002

Pierrick Gaudry, Florian Hess, and Nigel P. Smart, “Constructive and Destructive Facets of Weil Descent on Elliptic Curves,” *Journal of Cryptology* 15(1), 19–46, 2002. DOI: 10.1007/s00145-001-0011-x.

## Result used here

[CITED] For $E:y^2+xy=x^3+ax^2+b$ over $\mathbb F_{q^n}$ in characteristic two, the construction defines the magic number as the $\mathbb F_2$-dimension of the span of $(1,\sigma^i(\sqrt b))$ and, under its regularity condition, maps $E(\mathbb F_{q^n})$ to the Jacobian of a hyperelliptic curve over $\mathbb F_q$ of genus $2^{m-1}$ or $2^{m-1}-1$.

## What remains open here

The present experiment computes the genus invariant but does not reproduce the function-field fixed-field construction or the point-to-divisor map.
