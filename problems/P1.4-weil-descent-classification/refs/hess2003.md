# Hess 2003

Florian Hess, “The GHS Attack Revisited,” *Advances in Cryptology — EUROCRYPT 2003*, LNCS 2656, 374–387. DOI: 10.1007/3-540-39200-9_23.

## Result used here

[CITED] Hess gives an exact genus formula for the Artin–Schreier compositum underlying GHS. In the ordinary binary specialization with one coefficient equal to $1$, it becomes $g=2^{m-1}-2^{m-r}+1$, where $r$ is the conjugate-span dimension and $m$ is the pair-span dimension.
