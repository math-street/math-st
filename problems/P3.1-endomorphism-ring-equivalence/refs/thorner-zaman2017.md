# Thorner and Zaman (2017)

Jesse Thorner and Asif Zaman, “An explicit bound for the least prime ideal in the Chebotarev density theorem,” *Algebra & Number Theory* 11(5), 1135--1197 (2017); arXiv:1604.01750v2.

- [CITED] Theorem 1.1 gives an unconditional explicit fixed-power bound for a least prime in a Chebotarev class.
- [CITED] Theorem 1.2 proves that every primitive positive-definite binary quadratic form of discriminant $D$ represents a prime $\ll |D|^{694}$.
- [PROVED] These least-prime bounds do not supply the inverse-polylogarithmic density needed by Wesolowski's expected-polynomial-time samplers.
