# Kannan (1987)

- [CITED] Ravi Kannan, "Minkowski's convex body theorem and integer programming," *Mathematics of Operations Research* 12(3), 415--440 (1987).
- [CITED] The paper gives shortest- and closest-vector algorithms using \(n^{O(n)}\) arithmetic operations on an \(n\)-dimensional input of bit length \(s\).
- [PROVED] In fixed rank four this supplies the polynomial-time CVP subroutine needed to extend Wesolowski's Voronoi lattice sampler from rank two to rank four.
