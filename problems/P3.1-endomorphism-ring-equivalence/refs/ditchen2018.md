# Ditchen (2018)

- [CITED] Jakob J. Ditchen, "On the average distribution of primes represented by binary quadratic forms," arXiv:1312.1502v1, manuscript dated 2018.
- [CITED] Theorems 1.1--1.2 average prime-representation errors over negative fundamental discriminants and form classes.
- [CITED] Corollary 1.8 gives fixed-power least-prime bounds for all classes for most discriminants, and a smaller exponent for most classes for most discriminants.
- [PROVED] These averages do not control the pushforward of a uniform Brandt ideal class through the deterministic LLL-and-gcd binary restriction in Wesolowski's Proposition 3.5.
