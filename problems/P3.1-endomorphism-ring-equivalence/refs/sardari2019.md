# Sardari (2019)

Naser T. Sardari, “The least prime number represented by a binary quadratic form,” arXiv:1803.03218v2 (2019).

- [CITED] Theorem 1.1 and Corollary 1.3 give unconditional lower bounds on the proportion of form/ideal classes representing a prime in a chosen interval.
- [PROVED] The result is averaged over classes and therefore does not certify the adversarial fixed norm form appearing in Wesolowski's Theorem 3.7.
