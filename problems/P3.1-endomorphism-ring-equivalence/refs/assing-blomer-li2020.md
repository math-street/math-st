# Assing, Blomer, and Li (2020)

Edgar Assing, Valentin Blomer, and Junxian Li, “Uniform Titchmarsh divisor problems,” arXiv:2005.13915v1 (2020).

- [CITED] Theorem 2.1 gives an unconditional estimate when the auxiliary moduli are bounded by a fixed power of $\log x$.
- [CONDITIONAL: GRH] The same theorem permits moduli bounded by $x^{\delta'}$ and gives a power-saving error under GRH.
- [PROVED] Wesolowski 2022 needs the latter range because its modulus parameter includes $b=p$ while only $\log n=\operatorname{poly}(\log p)$ is allowed.
