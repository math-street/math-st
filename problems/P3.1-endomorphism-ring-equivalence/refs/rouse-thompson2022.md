# Rouse and Thompson (2022)

- [CITED] Jeremy Rouse and Katherine Thompson, "Quaternary quadratic forms with prime discriminant," arXiv:2206.00412v1 (2022).
- [CITED] Theorem 1 gives an explicit representation-number lower bound for positive-definite quaternary forms whose Gram determinant is a prime.
- [PROVED] The theorem does not directly cover normalized quaternion ideal norm forms, whose discriminant is \(p^2\), or the index-\((8\ell)^3\) residue sublattices used in A003.
