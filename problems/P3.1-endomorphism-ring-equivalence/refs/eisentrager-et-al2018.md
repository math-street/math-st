# Eisenträger et al. (2018)

Kirsten Eisenträger, Sean Hallgren, Kristin E. Lauter, Travis Morrison, and Christophe Petit, “Supersingular isogeny graphs and endomorphism rings: reductions and solutions,” EUROCRYPT 2018 Part III, LNCS 10822, 329--368, doi:10.1007/978-3-319-78372-7_11.

- [CONDITIONAL: GRH] Proposition 1 uses effective Chebotarev to choose $q=O((\log p)^2)$ when $p\equiv1\pmod8$.
- [CONDITIONAL: GRH] Proposition 3 computes the special curve/dictionary in polynomial time because that $q$ is polylogarithmic in $p$.
- [CITED] The remaining quaternion-path reductions in the paper use KLPT distribution heuristics; Wesolowski 2022 replaces those heuristics conditionally on GRH.
