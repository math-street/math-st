# Goren and Love (2025)

- [CITED] Eyal Z. Goren and Jonathan R. Love, "On elements of prescribed norm in maximal orders of a quaternion algebra," *Canadian Journal of Mathematics* 77(6), 1938--1965 (2025); arXiv:2307.16828.
- [CITED] The paper records that the theta series of a maximal order norm lattice in \(B_{p,\infty}\) is a weight-two modular form of level \(\Gamma_0(p)\).
- [CITED] It also records that integral quaternion lattices with maximal left and right orders are locally similar to maximal-order lattices.
- [PROVED] These facts support the level estimate used in A003; the paper itself does not provide the prime-valued vector sampler proposed there.
