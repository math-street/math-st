Tsz Hon Yuen, Sherman S. M. Chow, Huangting Wu, Cong Zhang, and Siu-Ming Yiu,
“Exponent-Inversion P-Signatures and Accountable Identity-Based Encryption
from SXDH,” *IACR Communications in Cryptology* 1(3), article 48, 2024.
DOI: 10.62056/ahsdkmp-3y.

Primary page: <https://cic.iacr.org/p/1/3/48>

## Extracted statement

[CITED] The authors construct dual-form Boneh–Boyen-like signatures and
Gentry-IBE variants with proofs under SXDH.

[CITED] The result changes the schemes; it is not a reduction of the original
$q$-SDH assumption to SXDH.
