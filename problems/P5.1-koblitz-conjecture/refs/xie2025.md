# Xie 2025 -- almost-prime orders over prime-power fields

**Reference:** Likun Xie, "Almost Prime Orders of Elliptic Curves Over Prime Power Fields," arXiv:2504.18732, 2025.

- [CITED] The paper studies bounded-almost-prime values of $\#E(\mathbb F_{p^\ell})/\#E(\mathbb F_p)$ for prime $\ell\ge2$. (Abstract.)
- [CITED] Its CM results for $y^2=x^3-x$ are unconditional, while the analogous non-CM results use GRH and effective Chebotarev. (Abstract.)
- [CITED] Its vector-sieve lower bound concerns a square-free almost-prime quotient over $\mathbb F_{p^2}$, not primality of $\#E(\mathbb F_p)/8$. (Abstract.)

**What it leaves open:**

- [CITED] The fixed-curve prime-order asymptotic in the original Koblitz problem. (Abstract.)
