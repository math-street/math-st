# Dey--Saha--Sivaraman--Vatwani 2025 -- conditional refined constant

**Reference:** Sampa Dey, Arnab Saha, Jyothsnaa Sivaraman, and Akshaa Vatwani, "On the refined Koblitz conjecture," *Journal of Mathematical Analysis and Applications* 546(1), article 129212, 2025, DOI 10.1016/j.jmaa.2024.129212.

- [CITED] For a fixed non-CM elliptic curve and integer quotient $t$, the paper targets the asymptotic count of primes for which $N_p/t$ is prime. (Abstract.)
- [CITED] Its determination of the refined Koblitz constant assumes both an elliptic analogue of the Elliott--Halberstam conjecture and a conjecture on the average growth of $N_p$. (Abstract.)
- [CITED] The result is conditional and therefore does not settle the unconditional fixed-curve Koblitz conjecture. (Abstract.)

**What it changes:**

- [CITED] It gives a sharper description of sufficient conjectural distribution inputs than the earlier GRH-based almost-prime and upper-bound results. (Dey et al. 2025; David and Wu 2012.)

**What it leaves open:**

- [CITED] Removing both conjectural inputs while retaining the fixed-curve prime-value asymptotic. (Abstract.)
