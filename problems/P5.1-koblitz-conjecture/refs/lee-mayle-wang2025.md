# Lee--Mayle--Wang 2025 -- average congruence-class biases

**Reference:** Sung Min Lee, Jacob Mayle, and Tian Wang, "Opposing average congruence class biases in the cyclicity and Koblitz conjectures for elliptic curves," *Canadian Journal of Mathematics*, published online 2025, DOI 10.4153/S0008414X25101156; arXiv:2408.16641.

- [CITED] The paper states that Zywina's refined Koblitz conjecture remains open. (Abstract.)
- [CITED] It formulates Koblitz constants for primes in arithmetic progressions and proves moment results for those constants over curve families. (Abstract and introduction.)
- [CITED] Those average results do not prove the prime-order asymptotic for a single fixed elliptic curve. (Abstract.)

**What it leaves open:**

- [CITED] The unconditional positive-constant asymptotic for one fixed curve. (Abstract.)
